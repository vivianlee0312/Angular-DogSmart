Top 10 Dog Breeds

The Canada Dog Association released their stats on the most popular dog breeds in Canada. It further gives us the data for the number of dogs in Canada that are avalialbe for aduption. It is categorized by thier age group: baby (under 1yr), young (1~3yrs), adult (3~8yrs), and senior (8yrs +).

This interface represents the 10 most popular dog breeds from 2014 to 2016.
